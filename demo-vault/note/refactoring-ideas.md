# Community “staples”

Ceremonies that grow the community

---

- ☑️ New members — bring them in in via survey + 1:1 onboarding
- ☑️ Say Hello — make everyone introduce themselves
  - ⬜ Check in with those who sign up but do not say hello
- ☑️ Weekly connections — curated intros for new members
- ☑️ Weekly events — community chats + expert talks
  - ☑️ Organize Thursday chats
  - ⬜ Surface expert talks within the community
  - ⬜ Bring in external guests to have talks
- ☑️ Coming up — make people participate to articles
- ⬜ Resurrect — check in with people who have been inactive
