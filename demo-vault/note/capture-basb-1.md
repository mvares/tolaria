# Capture (BASB #1)

## What to capture

- Inspiring
  - Is this something that could inspire me in the future?
- Useful
  - Could this be useful for future projects
- Surprising
  - Does this violate my expectations or go against my mental models?
  - Claude Shannon: “Information is what surprises you”
- Personal
  - Is this hard-won knowledge gained through personal experience (by you)?
  - Kinds
    - Stories
    - Experiences
    - Insights
    - Memories
## How to capture (Capture Tools)

- Notes Apps
- Social Media (favourites, bookmarks, …)
- Read Later
  - Instapaper + IFTTT
  - Pocket + Readwise
- Ebook
  - Kindle + Readwise
- Web Clippers
- Voice memos
  - Otter.ai
- Save everything to Evernote
  - Universal Inbox concept
