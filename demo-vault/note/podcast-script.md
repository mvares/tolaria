# Podcast Script

## Script

#### Introduction

Objectives: 

1. Gain viewer attention in the first 1-2 minutes by focusing on the interview main clip and providing all the details about the guest, the key points of the episode with the support of onscreen titles.

1. Provide a full interview with introduction (as above), sponsor, chapters and call-to-action.

 

#### Episode

- **Guest Clip**
*Duration*: 1 min max
*Content*: Interview excerpt, onscreen text regarding the most important concepts; Guest name and role/qualification.


- **Luca’s Interview Introduction**
*Duration*: 1 min max
*Content*: Luca’s recording introducing the guest and the main key points discussed during the episode (max 3-4, as we always did in the previous seasons); Key points highlighted by onscreen text, short cut from the episode in split screen without audio during guest introduction in order to show also his/her appearance.


- **Luca’s Podcast/Refactoring Introduction
***Duration*: (30 sec - 1 min)
*Content*: Luca’s own introduction as an engineer and the founder/main writer of Refactoring as a brand; Luca’s recording cut with screen recording of Refactoring brand offer (newsletter, article archive, community, YouTube channel, Spotify Podcast channel).


- **Sponsor**
*Duration*: As needed
*Content*: Sponsor related video with Luca’s audio recording and onscreen images/videos according to the current sponsor.


- **Interview
***Duration*: Full interview length
*Content*: Video edit with single cam (Luca/Guest) and split screen with chapters.


- **Ending**
*Duration*: 10-20 seconds
*Content*: Call-to-action to funnel viewers to other podcast/YouTube content and Refactoring newsletter subscription.
