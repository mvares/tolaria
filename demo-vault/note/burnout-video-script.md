# Burnout video script

- Intro

- Mastermind about burnout

- Creeps up gradually

- Boiling frog

- Gradually lose pieces of what makes you a happy and energetic person

- Lesser version of yourself

- My time as a founder

- Then employee, now creator

- Now more sensitive to how I feel and how I perform

- We’ll cover

### What triggers burnout

- Official definition by the WHO

- Christina Maslach

- Workload

- Control

- Reward

- Community

- Fairness

- Values

### How to spot burnout in yourself

- Daily awareness

- Periodic surveying

- Listen to your body and routines

- Ask for an outside perspective

### Spotting burnout in your team

- Daily awareness -> 1:1s & casual chat

- Periodic surveying

- Listen to your body -> Listen for changes

### Recovery

- Journaling

- Change your environment

- Physical recovery

- Social connection

- Take time off
