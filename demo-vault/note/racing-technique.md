# Racing Technique

## Braking / accelerating

- You brake until the Apex, reducing brake pressure towards the apex
  - Trail braking
- You throttle after apex, gradually
- If you accelerate before apex = entered too slow
- If you smash the throttle you reduce a lot grip
  - Good technique significantly affects grip
- We don’t want to move the platform of the car too quickly
## Brake bias

- Add brake bias in fast corners where you still need to brake a bit - lots of percorrenza, eg sunset at Sebring
- Reduce bb in deep braking zones
- Adjust bb for cold tires
  - Some cars understeer others oversteer
- Cars without abs especially sensitive to bb
  - If u lock fronts -> reduce bb
  - If you lose rear -> increase bb
- Adjust max 2% either direction
