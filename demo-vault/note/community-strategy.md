# Community Strategy

## 3️⃣ Three types of tasks

---

- 💬 Community activities — public, members-facing stuff
- 🗃️ Backoffice — stuff we do behind the curtain
- 🔨 Quality of life — improvements to organization / workflows (e.g. improve video quality)
## 🪶 Light vs Deep interactions

---

- Create both light and deep types of interaction
  - 🪶 Light — little time involved by participants
  - 🧱 Deep — more time, more thoughtful
- Idea: people get onboarded with light activities and gradually move to participating to more deep activities
- Feeling: right now we are too skewed towards of deep activities
## ⭐ Principles

---

- Recurring — make things predictable rather than one off.
- Personal — favour personal interactions, like reaching out to people 1:1.
- Ambassadors — involve ambassadors. Make them own some activities.
- Content — favour initiatives from which we can extract insights and ideas for content

## 🗃️ Backoffice tasks

---

- Member outreach — to prompt participation / introducing themselves
- Member invites — personally invite people who reply to the survey to the community
- Update Airtable — tag members’ info on Airtable
## 🔨 Quality of life improvements

---

- Improve video quality of calls (change tool)
- Reduce number of spaces for confusion
## 🔄 Workflow

---

- We create initiatives as experiments.
  - For each experiment we:
    - Set KPIs for success
    - Get feedback from people
  - Outcomes
    - We keep doing those that are successful
    - We iterate on those that are promising
    - We discard those that don’t work
