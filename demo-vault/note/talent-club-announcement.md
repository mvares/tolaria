# Talent Club announcement

Hey everyone! This is a sneak peek into something new I am building for Refactoring 💥

I wanted to give you early access and also ask for your feedback!

It is called “Talent Club” and it’s a product to help Refactoring members find exciting gigs, with zero effort.

Basically, members can apply to join a pool of people who are open to receive job offers, by entering info about themselves and the type of job opportunities they would be interested in (role, location, expected salary, etc.).

On the other side, companies can apply (I review them) to access this pool of candidates, and contact 1:1 those who are a good fit for their open positions.

The value prop should be clear for both:

- Developers / Managers receive high quality job opportunities 1:1, with zero effort on their side. And they can totally opt out anytime.
- Companies access a high quality pool of candidates, saving time on their hiring.
What do you think? Does it make sense to you? 🤔

Here is a link to sign up if you are interested: https://refactoring-jobs.pallet.com/talent/welcome?referral=true

Let me know your thoughts!
