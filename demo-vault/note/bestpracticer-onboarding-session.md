### Sessions 1-3

- Average covering 2 skills from our curriculum per session
- Assign homework - curriculum and/or on-the-job actions
- Get the learner behavior of bringing challenges to the sessions
- Create a Magic Moment by Session 3: Identify an on-the-job win based on a skill discussed during a prior session
### Sessions 5-7

- Do a goal check-in - return to the goals from onboarding, review outcomes. Create new goals or continue existing ones
