# Refactoring Key Ideas

#### **1) Speed is all that matters**

- no tradeoff between speed and stability

#### **2) Generalists vs specialists**

- generalists build product & scale throughput

- specialists build platform & scale quality

#### **3) What’s good for humans is good for AI**

- good practices are easier and more valuable

- delegate boring work to AI and expand your scope

#### **4) Extreme delegation**

- human judgment -> procedure -> automation / AI / constraint

- revert instead of approve
