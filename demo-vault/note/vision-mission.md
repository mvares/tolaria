# Vision & Mission

- Refactoring helps engineering teams to improve their work
- Through:
  - Articles — wisdom and best practices, written and curated by Luca.
  - Community — connect with a professional network of peers and mentors for advice, sharing ideas, give and receive help.
  - Deals — recommend and provide discounts for the best developer tools available.
  - Jobs — provide trusted companies a way to connect with engineering talent, and engineers a way to receive valuable job offers.
## Tagline

Making software, together

## Mission

The mission of Refactoring is to help people make great software, together.

Just like we make software together, this community is about learning together how to make it — leveraging our collective experiences, insights, and unique perspectives.

Refactoring started with my own articles. I will continue to write them week after week, but I am just one guy and at some point there is a ceiling to what I can draw from my own experience.

This community is the place where we all contribute to creating a larger wisdom about building software, working with people, and being happy with our work.

Refactoring is going to be the best engineering community in the world. 

Welcome on board! 🚢
