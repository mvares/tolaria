# 12 Questions

1. 🔨 Create – How can I create something new that uses my skills and passion, and make it grow over the years?
1. 🧠 Learn – How can I keep my skills and knowledge aligned with my vision of the future of society?
1. 👥 Connect – How can I make myself surrounded of brilliant people coming from different backgrounds (e.g. entrepreneurship, engineering, art)?
1. 🤝 Assist – How can I help several people improving their life, addressing an important problem of society?
1. 🏡 Family – How can I develop a happy family with my spouse and children?
1. 🏡 Family – How can I best support and stay close to my parents and relatives?
1. 🍻 Friends – How can I develop profound, durable relationships with my friends?
1. 🏦 Earn – How can I build a position where I can afford taking professional risks without impacting the lifestyle of my family and myself?
1. 💪 Health – How can I stay in perfect physical and psychological health? How can I create a process that maintains and improves my personal health?
1. 🎸 Music – How can I stay interested in making music and find joy in doing it?
1. 🎒 Process – How can I develop a process to make me progress reliably on my goals, while also leaving space for creativity and serendipity?
1. 🎒 Process – How can I design a life that makes me happy and grateful, while pursuing my passions?
