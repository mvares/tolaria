# iRacing Lessons / Notes

### Goals of each race (priority order)

1. Finish the race
1. Finish the race with as little incidents as possible
1. Finish the race in a good position
### Lessons Learned

- Stay focused until after race
- Don’t try to capitalize on crashes
- Do a good, safe start
- Survive first lap
- Don’t race when you are tired
