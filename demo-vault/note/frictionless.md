## The three elements of devex

- Reducing **cognitive load** to free mental capacity for complex challenges

- Optimizing **feedback loops** to accelerate decision-making

- Protecting **flow state** to maximize deep work

#### Cognitive load

- the amount of mental processing required for a developer to perform a task.

- team topologies

- systematic reduciton of unnecessary complexity

#### Feedback loop

- Little’s law

#### Flow state

- complete immersion in an activity with energized focus and personal enjoyment.

- Effective feedback loops are crucial for maintaining flow.

- Autonomy contributes to flow bc of less intrruptions / coordination

## Improving DevEx — 7-step process

Iterative process (cycle multiple times):

- Steps 1-2 — quick wins: identify most disruptive friction points across the three elements

- Steps 3-4 — use data to measure impact of friction, develop strategy to address highest-impact areas first

- ~~Steps 5-6 — sell your strategy to stakeholders~~

- Step 7 — tools for evaluating progress

### 1) Listening tour

- Interview all developers

#### Identify themes

- E.g. process confusion, missing docs, workflow interruptions, …

- Also use AI to identify patterns / categories

- If you have existing data, use it

#### Visualize workflow

- List the steps devs have to do to write and ship code

- Path to production mapping

#### Understand stakeholders

#### Share what you learned

### 2) Start small & get a quick win

- RICE for DevEx

- Consider the feedback loop — if communication feels missing

#### Share early wins to gain momentum

- Fail fast and share learnings

- Celebrate wins along the way

- Share credit and findings

- Keep the conversation going

### 3) Use data to optimize

- Start with listening tour

- Qualitative vs quantitative — self-reported vs telemetry ([table](https://share.cleanshot.com/20gyy9T1))

- Identify what you already have

- Use surveys for fast insights

- Simple devex survey (15 mins max, once per quarter)

- Data strategy: work backward from problems to metrics

### 4) Decide strategy and priority

- Refine RICE approach with data

### 5) Sell your strategy

- Manage stakeholders, customize message, etc.

### 6) Drive change at your scale

- Create cultural shift

- Technical foundation must provide clear value to developers

- Find champions

- Run focused pilots

- Document successes and learnings

- Build momentum

- Create a clear feedback loop

### 7) Evaluate progress + show value

- Analyze data

- Communicate results

- Share progress

- Navigate conflicting metrics

- Learn and improve
