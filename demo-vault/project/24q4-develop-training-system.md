## KPIs

- Lean Body Mass (LBM)
- Body Fat Percentage (BFP)
## Goals (90th percentile)

- VO2 Max = 45 (33)
  - Measure once a month
- Lean Body Mass = 75kg (60)
  - Total weight = ~83kg
  - Measure once a week
- Body Fat Percentage = 10% (13)
  - Measure once a week
## Routine

- 3 days / week
- 1 day cardio
  - Rowing / Elliptical
- 2 days strength
  - Push ups
---

## 🚴 Cardio training

- Elliptical / Rowing — 15 mins, once a week
## 🏋️‍♂️ Strength training

### Workout A (30 minutes per week)

Focus: Upper body (push and pull) + core.

- Push-ups — 3 sets of 8-12 reps
- Pull-ups — 3 sets of 6-8 reps
- Dumbbell Rows — 3 sets of 8-10 reps per arm.
- Plank — 2 sets of 30-60 seconds.
### Workout B (30 minutes per week)

Focus: Upper body push and pull + dumbbell work for lower body.

- Push-ups — 3 sets of 8-12 reps
- Pull-ups — 3 sets of 6-8 reps
- Goblet Squats — 3 sets of 8-12 reps
- Dumbbell Overhead Press — 3 sets of 8-10 reps
