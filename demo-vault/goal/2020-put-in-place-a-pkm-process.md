# ✅ [2020] Put in place a PKM process

I see PKM as composed by several major elements:

### Action

The action part is about goals, projects, planning and executing. This is where Notion excels. 

Vision → x4 Year → x4 Season → x12 Week

Journaling

### Note Taking

The whole Zettelkasten workflow, that I would like to adopt as it is. It doesn't need to be connected to the action part: it can live in a separate tool, like Roam or some offline thing. It should be optimized for focus, and for being eternal.

### Resources

Resources might literally be anything: topics, useful links, business tools, people. They are lists of things, usually connected to other things, and for this reason I believe Notion is the best tool for them. Notion has also a web clipper for fast input.

---

### LifeOS

- Action Management
  - Time and goals: Vision → Year → Quarter (Season) → Week
  - Purposes
  - Areas
  - Projects
  - Tasks
- Resources Management
  - Lists of things you want to categorize, put aside and reference.
    - People, books, movies, tools 
- Knowledge Management (Zettelkasten)
  - Fleeting Notes
  - Literature Notes
  - Evergreen Notes
