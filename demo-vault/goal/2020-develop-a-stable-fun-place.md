# ❌ [2020] Develop a stable fun place

I am someone who pushes hard on himself. I am used to it and I believe this is mostly a force for good. 

However, I also believe it is great to sit back from time to time, and save time for some personal hobby, or game, that has no goals attached. No expectations. Just enjoy the moment.

This is not necessarily something that I should do alone. It could be something I share with Flavia, or Andrea, or my friends. But the #1 requirements is that I do it for my own enjoyment, not for some greater good.

I want to develop this space and make sure it has a place in my life.
