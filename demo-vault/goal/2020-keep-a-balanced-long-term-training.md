# ✅ [2020] Keep a balanced, long-term training

In the last 3 months I have trained like never before. Now, I feel I am reaching a plateau, and I am not sure anymore that what I do is the best I can do for my health and fitness. 

I don't think it is possible to come up with a plan that will stay the same for more than – say – three months at a time, because I want to grow and progress, and with progress comes change.

However, I can develop principles and KPIs that will guide the plan revisions, setting goals quarter by quarter.
