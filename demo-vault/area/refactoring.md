# Refactoring
Running the Refactoring business. Some key parts:

## What is Refactoring
Newsletter and media company focused on engineering leadership and management. Founded and run by Luca Rossi, it helps tech leaders make their teams ship faster and better.
## Key Numbers
As of February 2026:
- 174,000+ subscribers (largest newsletter in engineering management space)
- 1,500+ members in private community
- 300+ articles in the archive (5 years of content)
- 55+ episodes of the podcast
## Content Format
The newsletter follows a weekly cadence:
### 💡 Monday Ideas (Free)
3 actionable ideas to get better at your job, sent every Monday.
### 🧵 Wednesday Essays (Paid)
Original, in-depth articles on engineering topics, backed by experience, research, and case studies.
### 🎙️ Friday Podcast (Bi-weekly)
Interviews with world-class engineering leaders. Past guests include:
- DHH (Ruby on Rails, Basecamp)
- Kent Beck (TDD, Extreme Programming)
- Guillermo Rauch (Vercel)
- Gonçalo Silva (Todoist)
- Thiago Ghisi, Laura Tacho, and many more
## Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Subscriptions** - monthly ($15/month) or annual (~$150/year)  membership
2. **Sponsorships** - Ads and content partnerships across all formats

### Sponsorship Types
Refactoring offers sponsorship opportunities across three main formats:
- **Newsletter Ads**: Sponsor ads in Monday Ideas editions
- **Podcast Ads**: Sponsor ads in episodes
- **Guest posts**: Essays written in collaboration with sponsor companies, published as wednesday essays
