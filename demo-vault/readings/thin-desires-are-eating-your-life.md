# Thin Desires Are Eating Your Life

We are living with a near-universal thin desire: wanting something that cannot actually be gotten, that we can't define, from a source that has no interest in providing it. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd57326m1cdn86yy7fghxkbc))

The distinction between thick and thin desires isn't original to me. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd573kncfptdghms0q7zw1wq))

A thick desire is one that changes you in the process of pursuing it.
A thin desire is one that doesn't. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd573de3vr9023gneb0a6f57))

The thin desire reproduces itself without remainder.
The thick desire transforms its host. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd57441408abcvmae84f3v3v))

The business model of most consumer technology is to identify some thick desire, find the part of it that produces a neurological reward, and then deliver that reward without the rest of the package. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd574qx8hje72ngf13nrqqkf))

Social media gives you the feeling of social connection without the obligations of actual friendship. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd574v5br2sxh0pzvpdkf1rk))

Pornography gives you sexual satisfaction without the vulnerability of partnership.
Productivity apps give you the feeling of accomplishment without anything being accomplished. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd575162q9bd6hy1sbmqncez))

Thick desires are inconvenient.
They take years to cultivate and can't be satisfied on demand.
The desire to master a craft, to read slowly, to be embedded in a genuine community, to understand your place in some tradition larger than yourself: these desires are effortful to acquire and impossible to fully gratify. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd57674a8kvv8r8vqfb5p41v))

The thick life doesn't scale. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd575wg8hzjn4d1gxj2tt0k1))

Build something that will never scale, never be monetized, never attract users. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kd576r7bey2gjg1ch9bk4q7f))
