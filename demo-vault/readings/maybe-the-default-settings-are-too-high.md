# Maybe the Default Settings Are Too High

I’ve been reading *Lord of the Rings* for two months ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh1409tfrv5t6166yw6h9k3))

From the beginning, I’ve read the whole thing aloud. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh143609yqzpwzdvnjxzy0z))

I tried slowing down *even more*, and discovered something. I slowed to a pace that felt almost absurd, treating each sentence as though it might be a particularly important one. I gave each one maybe triple the usual time and attention, ignoring the fact that there are hundreds of pages to go.
This leisurely pace made Middle-Earth blossom before my eyes. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh14gyhk74qpk44pg2qtydx))

What’s paradoxical is that it’s precisely the seeking of that “good stuff” that normally drives me to eat so quickly, and miss most of what I’m seeking. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh15x963np618dqsvmway0h))

When you try to barrel ahead to access the good stuff quicker, you get less of it in the end. Slow down and much more of it is released. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh1646zrb4cerpkccvdq679))

Both are analogous to slowing down while you vacuum a carpet. If you pass the vacuum head too quickly, you miss half the dirt. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh16sczaq2a6jrj9nzpdm1v))

I also suspect that modern living, with its infinite supply of consumables, tends to push our rate-of-intake dials too high. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh17fh7f1kpjnfgnr7ws85k))

It might sound like I’m just offering clichés – less is more, stop and smell the roses, take your time – and I guess I am. But clichés suffer the same issue: they are often profound insights, consumed and passed on too rapidly for their real meaning to register anymore. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh18c6vvg0wwm73h0pekt8a))

Slowing down your rate of consumption will inevitably change what you *want* to consume. Reading throwaway news articles or AI slop with great care and attention is only going to show you how empty of value it is. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01kdh1a00wqcj1qjdxrwnen98k))
