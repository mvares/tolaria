# Collaboration Sucks

Being good at feedback means knowing when not to give it ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa7wf94y83076ffrx21wbd))

“You’re the driver” is a [key value](https://posthog.com/handbook/values?utm_source=posthog-newsletter&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=collaboration-sucks) for us at PostHog. We aim to hire people who are great at their jobs and get out of their way. No deadlines, minimal coordination, and no managers telling you what to do.
In return, we ask for extraordinarily high ownership and the ability to get a lot done by *yourself.* Marketers ship code, salespeople answer technical questions without backup, and [product engineers](https://posthog.com/blog/what-is-a-product-engineer?utm_source=posthog-newsletter&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=collaboration-sucks) work across the stack. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa8ekhyyckntffta4mja5b))

This means there is almost always someone better at what you are doing than you are. It is tempting to get them, or anybody really, involved and ✨ collaborate ✨, but collaboration forces the driver to slow down and explain stuff ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa8rqvk1d8tfdmnhxy1ytr))

This *sometimes* leads to valuable insights, but *always* slows the driver down. It erodes their motivation, confidence, and effectiveness, and ultimately leads us to ship less. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa8zcs1e17fkes0kb7a5sx))

If collaboration sucks, why do people do it? ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa96wyv68dqx1mswzvxdbs))

People want to be helpful. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa9hmaa69p0grsymznj401))

People aren’t specific enough about what feedback they need. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa9kbsxb133crx04a5gyc5))

When someone has a good idea, the response often defaults to “let’s discuss” ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9waa237j7e3qda6684jgxyt))

People just want to talk about stuff because they are too busy can’t be bothered to act on it. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9wa9yw4edcvyvg9ewy4ezh3))

How to crush collaboration (and go farther, faster) ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9waaaanjgg09pdzdg4yb7qn))

Default to shipping. Pull requests > issues > Slack messages. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9waacm4araj17sdfw4qkeyp))

Every time you see ✨ collaboration ✨ happening, speak up and destroy it. Say “there are too many people involved. X, you are the driver, you decide.” ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k9waapvfk7yhbep66r2vt4e5))
