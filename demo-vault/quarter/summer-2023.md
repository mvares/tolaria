## 🔍 Review

This quarter was one of the most important of my life — and I am terribly late at reviewing it, I hope I will be able to recollect events and feelings.

The whole quarter was mostly filled up by the personal trifecta:

1. 👰 I got married with Flavia Angelini 👏
1. ✈️ We went 3 weeks+ on honeymoon in Polynesia + California
1. 🏡 We relocated to the villa in Via Aurelia Antica
All these three things ended up being runaway successes.

The wedding was beautiful, informal and intimate as we hoped for. I sang the song for Flavia, and she declared a small poem. Guests loved the ceremony and the whole party. I also did a small serenade at home with friends a few days the wedding. 

Our honeymoon was beautiful too. Polynesia was a bit underwhelming, because of bad weather and some logistic issues, but we liked it nonetheless. California was extraordinary. We loved every minute of it: the itinerant trip by car, parks, small towns, and san francisco.

In early August we relocated to the new house, and it’s been incredible so far. It feels like the final step up and I can literally see us living here forever. I am writing this in November so we are two months in the next quarter already, so I will save more bits about this for next quarter’s review. The Sestriere situation is a bit tricky and we have lawyers involved, but honestly who cares? Things will sort out in a way or another.

The months leading to the wedding were heavy: I had to work twice as hard to prepare articles in advance, and didn’t have much room for pretty much anything else. Nonetheless, Refactoring grew just fine and we are — incredibly — on track for the end of the year goals. 

Sponsorships are going great, and both free and paid growth is on fire. Sent 50% discounts to old free subs and it converted more than 100 folks!

On the personal side, I had a great trip with friends to Palma de Mallorca for the bachelor’s party, Claudio & Alessandra got married, and I got surprisingly closer to Claudio, which I am happy of.

I also invested more on Masterworks and eToro, and personal finance is in a better shape than ever
