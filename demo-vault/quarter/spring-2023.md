### 🌀 Refactoring

This quarter has been amazing for Refactoring growth, to the point that we caught up with the year plan:

- Free subscribers grew a lot thanks to a good mix of recommendations, good ads, and organic.
- Paid subscribers grew a lot especially thanks to the increase of price to $15, which drove ~150 new paid subs before of the increase.
- Sponsorships went extremely well, at about ~$7000/months. The new process put in place is working.
I have also launched guides, the new branding, more video interviews, and in general fired from all cylinders!

This quarter was probably the best Refactoring quarter ever and I felt “I still got it” — I got what it takes to take things to the next level, in a game that gets harder and harder the more you go on.

### 🎒 Personal

The big news is moving to the new house in Via Aurelia Antica 480. It’s an enormous villa, closer to friends and family. It’s a big jump that is a bit scary in terms of lifestyle (economics shouldn’t change much), but I feel it is ultimately the right call. I am very attracted by the idea of building an office space where to invite friends and co-workers (e.g. Michele, Giorgio, etc.)

Wedding prep also went very well and we basically did everything.

I am playing a lot of chess, both with friends (Gabriele, Luca Mastro, Matteo Cellini) and alone. Almost got to 1200 ELO up from 800!

The combination of wedding prep, moving to the new house, and Refactoring took a toll on my work-life balance. I feel tired and unfocused, and I basically gave up any hobby — not driving on iRacing anymore since the Meta Quest broke, even if I bought a replacement.

Overall though I feel good and I understand this is a necessary struggle, and I have already plans to scale my work better.
