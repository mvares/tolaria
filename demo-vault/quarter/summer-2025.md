## 🔍 Review

This was probably the hardest quarter ever — at least since I started tracking them on Notion. But many good things also came out of it. Let’s unpack it.

### 🎒 Personal — return from the ashes

- ❤️ Relationship with Flavia has been hard
  - Our relationship has been tense and I have been very unsure about where our marriage was going.
  - Flavia has regularly felt detached, more focused on her work and friends, and she retreated from the everything we planned to do: sex and children specifically.  
  - We did several trips with mixed results
    - The trip to Maldives was not good — we argued a lot and Flavia didn’t enjoy her time there
    - We spent a weekend in Umbria that was meh
    - We went to Scotland for 4 days for our anniversary. It was the best trip out of the three, but it was also hard and we had a couple of hard (but positive) talks
  - I have learned that when my marriage is not doing well, a lot of the rest falls apart
    - I stopped working out in September, had worse sleep and less energy in general
    - I also felt less sense of purpose at work (more on that later)
  - Backtracking from the idea of having kids now really hit me
  - But all of this was also transformational — I learned to have more respect for myself, to speak out when needed, and to truly grow my relationship with Flavia as adults.
  - Today I am positive about our future even if I know it might be challenging, and I am ready to work for it
- ♟️ Chaotic hobbies
  - I did almost zero racing after GT Royale ended up poorly.
    - I don’t know how to fit racing into my life, and at this point I am not 100% I want to do it
  - Unexpectedly, I did incredibly well at FIFA, eventually winning the first division title
  - I got to 1500 ELO at chess, which feels like a fantastic achievement.
    - I am motivated by chess these days, but not to the point of hiring a coach or anything
    - I like doing it but I don’t have a strong “why” behind it
- 💻 Gear
  - I bought a lot of new gear: a display, a desk, a plant, lights, the autopole, and more.
    - I feel by the time things with Flavia got better and I found a new sense of self, it’s like I unclogged myself and I felt the need to start fresh
### 🌀 Refactoring — a new mission

- The Refactoring quarter has been messy bit it felt like a crucial step forward
- I created the Srl and started delegating more and more work to Matteo and Pier
  - It’s hard and it doesn’t always work, but it’s the only way forward
- Summer was the lowest point:
  - we kept bleeding paid subs
  - free growth was not great
  - I was not motivated by doing anything
- In September I started talking with Jordan from GrowthCommunity thanks to Matteo Colò, and I found new energy to find purpose and direction
  - This also coincided with the situation with Flavia getting better, and I found new motivation
- By the end of the quarter we defined a new mission: help teams ship fast — around which to orchestrate everything else
- I am excited to create a new $1000/year membership, and re-route the podcast and the newsletter around this
- Meanwhile sponsorships are going strong, which makes me confident to take this bet
