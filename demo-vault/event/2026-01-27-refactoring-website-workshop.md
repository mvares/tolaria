## Summary

Website design workshop with Mozestudio team. Discussed website objectives (newsletter sign-ups as primary conversion, attribution tracking for paid campaigns), Refactoring brand evolution (achieved professional quality upgrade while maintaining core vibes), current business structure (newsletter, community, podcast, course), and target audience (CTOs/VPs of small-medium engineering teams). Website should serve as coherent landing destination with clear value proposition and pricing visibility without overwhelming visitors.

## Key Points

- **Website objectives**: Newsletter sign-ups as main conversion metric; attribution tracking that Substack doesn't provide; platform for paid campaigns with lead magnets
- **Brand evolution success**: Achieved professional quality upgrade while maintaining Refactoring vibes; new visual assets, colors, fonts working well
- **Current business structure**:
  - Newsletter: Primary entry point
  - Community: $15/month with dedicated mobile app (unique differentiator)
  - Podcast: High-profile guests (DHH, Martin Fowler, etc.)
  - Course: 8-week cohort ($2,000/seat, 10 participants) - current format unsustainable
- **Target audience**: CTOs/VPs of small-medium engineering teams (10-15 people); product companies preferred; focus on team-level process optimization
- **Content pillars**: (1) Developer Experience (foundation), (2) AI implementation (amplifies good/bad practices), (3) Product Engineering (raising team autonomy ceiling)
- **Positioning shift**: More personal brand integration; increased face/photo presence; positioning as full-time researcher ("think tank" approach)
- **Trust building**: Leverage testimonials (hundreds available) and interview guests as social proof
- **Content strategy**: Show product quality through samples; avoid information overload with clean, direct messaging
- **Site architecture**: Newsletter (primary CTA), Community (dedicated page with app showcase), Podcast (overview page, not full archive), Pricing table visible but not primary focus
