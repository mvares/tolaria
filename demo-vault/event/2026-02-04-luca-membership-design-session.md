## Summary

Design session for new Refactoring membership product. Discussed target audience (senior/staff engineers and engineering managers transitioning to leadership), value proposition (help teams ship faster), delivery model (2 live sessions/month, community rituals, curated content), and pricing ($150/month or stair-step approach starting at $47-97). Key focus on product-market fit, sustainable delivery, and clear transformation outcomes.

## Key Points

- **Target audience**: Senior/staff engineers + engineering managers moving into leadership roles; best fit is small to mid-sized companies
- **Core problems**: People management without playbook, high-stakes decisions under ambiguity, organizational politics
- **Value proposition**: Help senior engineers/managers make better decisions, run smoother teams, feel in control
- **Delivery model**: 2 live sessions/month (discussion-based, not lectures), community rituals (weekly prompts, structured intros, 15-min breakouts), async content from existing writing
- **Content themes**: Engineering leadership, team processes, AI (curate, don't own)
- **Pricing discussion**: Initial recommendation $150/month; Luca concerned about industry norm of free knowledge sharing; alternative stair-step approach $47-97 to start
- **Success metric**: 200 members = $30k MRR (0.1% of email list)
- **Onboarding**: Clear content journey with quick wins, community introduction template, get answer within 7 days
- **Strategy**: Position as "make your team ship faster" vs generic career growth; quantifiable outcomes (save 2+ hours/week)
- **Leverage**: Use existing 5+ years of content breadth/depth as structured journeys (mini-courses vs random articles)
- **External practitioners**: Bring in experts for live sessions on AI and other topics; can double as podcast content
