### 🏅 Podcast Summaries

I know for a fact many people don’t have enough time to go through 1-hour interviews week in and week out. 

We have had transcripts on Substack from day one, but these are not enough — it’s still a 1-hour transcript!

So, starting in season two, for each interview we are going to extract the 4-5 most important takeaways of the conversation, and put them in a ~1000-words summary that you can read, instead of listening to the whole thing.

Monday Ideas will also start surfacing ideas from the podcast interviews, and will link back to the full interview.

Summaries won’t be AI-generated, like you see everywhere. These will be high-quality, human-written pieces, 100% Refactoring-style.

Also, summaries will be exclusive for paid subscribers, to thank them for their support 🙏

As a recap, podcast episodes will always be free for everybody, but paid subscribe will also be able to:

- 💬 **Join the interviews live through the community **— and ask live questions to guests.

- 🏅 **Get the 10-mins summary of each interview **— organized into neat, separate ideas.
