# Introducing the Refactoring Mastermind

### ✍️ Post

---

Hey everyone, this is a special edition to announce a new community initiative, called the **Refactoring Mastermind! 🧠**

From now on, we will hold monthly sessions where you can meet other members of the Community and tap into their **collective expertise and support**, to help you work through the challenges that you are facing.

![image](../assets/283e6a62d320491d.png)

Sessions will be held and facilitated by [**Joel Chippindale**](https://refactoring.circle.so/u/0b4d91f2?show_back_link=true)** **— Joel is a [**CTO coach**](https://monkeysthumb.co.uk/) who supports CTOs, engineers and managers to accelerate their development and grow their businesses. He is also an ex-CTO with over 20 years of experience building, scaling and leading software development teams.

We will hold the first session on **March 19,** **at** **17:00 CEST 🗓️**

Please come to the session with a **personal challenge** that you are facing at work that you would like support with. During the session there will be the opportunity to 1) share your challenges, and 2) share your wisdom and perspectives to support your peers.

Here is how to join:

#### **🔒 If you are a paid subscriber**

You can register to the event through the [community](https://community.refactoring.club/home).

If you haven’t signed up to the community yet, just reply to this email and I will send you a signup link.

#### 📬 **If you are a free subscriber**

Consider joining the full plan to get access to the community and all the awesome activities we do together. We have all kinds of events running every week — exclusive interviews, book reviews, and now mastermind sessions.

![image](../assets/d222d5867d36403d.png)

On the community you get the same kind of support you would get by spending hundreds of dollars per month on **professional coaching**, only for real cheap.
