# Solicit payments

## 🎯 Goal

- Make sure the payment for a deal has come through

## ➡️ Input

- Closed deal that the partner has not paid for yet

## ⬅️ Output

- Confirmation that the deal has been paid, or…

- … ping to the sponsor to pay for the deal

## 📋 Instructions

1. Check deals that haven’t been paid yet

1. Log in into Unicredit or [Revolut](https://business.revolut.com/)

1. Check latest movements

1. Find if any of the latest movements match an existing deal

1. For any sponsor that has not paid yet, write them to solicit payment (once a week)
