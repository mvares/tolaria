# Publish new essays in Community

## 🎯 Goal

- Publish new essays in the community after they went out on substack

## ➡️ Input

- New essay on substack and in the airtable

## ⬅️ Output

- Article sent as a thread in the community

## 📋 Instructions

- Take latest article from [Airtable](https://airtable.com/appC87jPdTo2Ox7N2/tblrkRYTG5iJQdTF9/viwPOX46yLMHp9b98?blocks=hide) or from Substack

- Publish in [community essays](https://community.refactoring.club/c/articles/)

## 💻 Tool access

- Community

## 📚 Book reviews

The procedure is the same as above with a couple of differences:

- The post has to be made in the [Book Reviews section of Community](https://community.refactoring.club/c/book-reviews/)

- The card thumbnail is already available in [Canva](https://www.canva.com/design/DAGBWrcjZ5g/c78AbrEjAgIbyaTfPjOjAg/edit)
