# Migrate HH subs to Refactoring

## 🎯 Goal

- Migrate new subscribers of Hybrid Hacker to Refactoring every two days

## 📋 Instructions

1. Go to the [filtered view](https://hybridhacker.email/publish/subscribers?fs=%5B%7B%22column%22%3A%22subscription_type%22%2C%22relation%22%3A%22%22%2C%22value%22%3A%22free%22%7D%2C%7B%22column%22%3A%22subscription_created_at%22%2C%22relation%22%3A%22_gt%22%2C%22value%22%3A%222025-05-25%22%7D%5D&_no_an=true) of Hybrid Hacker

1. From the “…” menu export subscribers with all the columns and download the csv

1. Go to the [subscribers page](https://refactoring.fm/publish/subscribers) on Refactoring

1. Click on add subscribers

1. Upload the csv

1. Unflag “send welcome email…” and flag “these people have opted-in…”

1. Click on import

## 💻 Tool access

- Substack admin access

- Hybrid Hacker admin access
