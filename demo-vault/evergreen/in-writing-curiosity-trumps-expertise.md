# In writing, curiosity trumps expertise

You don’t have to necessarily focus on what you are the most expert in. In fact, I have found that genuine curiosity / passion trumps pure expertise.

Think about it: over a long period of time, you will always run out of things to say that purely come from your expertise. **Curiosity is what makes you learn more** and continuously generate new things to say.

Some of the most successful newsletters today were started by people who where not experts by any means, and just wanted to learn more about a space. 

[Ben](https://twitter.com/bentossell) launched [Ben’s Bites](https://www.bensbites.co/) as a simple way for himself to keep up with the AI progress. He was no AI expert, but got to 80K readers in just a few months.
