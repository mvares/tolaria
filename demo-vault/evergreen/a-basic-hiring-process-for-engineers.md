# A basic hiring process for engineers

The best hiring process always depends on your company and the role you are hiring for. Something basic but solid might look like:

1. **📑 Screening interview**

1. **🔨 2x Technical Interview**

1. **🍻 Cultural Interview**

1. **💸 Offer**

For the technical side, you may have a *take-home *+ a follow-up interview where you discuss the results and ask the candidate to implement something more on top. If you dislike home assignments, you can go for a system design session followed by a pair programming one.

If you allocate the right time from your team, you can truly run this from *application* to *offer* in two weeks. It’s two interviews per week, plus sending out the offer.

A fast hiring process makes for a great impression and always closes more deals.
