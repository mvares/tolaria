# Thin vs thick desires

- A thick desire is one that changes you in the process of pursuing it.

- A thin desire is one that doesn't.

The thin desire keeps reproducing itself.

The thick desire transforms its host.

Thick desires are inconvenient. They take years to cultivate and can't be satisfied on demand. The desire to master a craft, to read slowly, to be embedded in a genuine community: these desires are effortful to acquire and impossible to fully gratify.
