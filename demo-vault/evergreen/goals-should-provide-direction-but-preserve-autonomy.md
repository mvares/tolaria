# Goals should provide direction but preserve autonomy

Good goals are about **what** should be achieved, without being prescriptive about **how** it should be achieved. 

They provide **direction** while preserving **autonomy**.

- [Untitled](https://www.notion.so/91695d7c9a3d4bab9c673b1e9a5dab47)

Goals should be concrete, combining **qualitative** direction with **quantitative** results. People should always stay responsible of *what* should be built to meet such goals.

You can find several goals examples [here](https://hypercontext.com/goal-examples), following the [OKR framework](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OKR).
