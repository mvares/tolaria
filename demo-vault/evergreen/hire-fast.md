# Hire fast

The whole hiring process at a big firm [like Google](https://candor.co/articles/interview-prep/google-interview-process-interview-questions) or Facebook easily lasts **2+ months** and requires between 4 and 10 interviews.

Can you be faster? Can you hire a person in **one week**? 

- [Untitled](https://www.notion.so/13555af5d134426fa515dd89e5e78189) 

**Speed always wins deals**, and that is true in hiring as well. 

Speed signals **conviction**, makes candidates feel valuable, and prevents them from getting in touch with other opportunities.

---
