# Hire outside the box

As a startup one of the ways to win at hiring is to look **outside the box**.

Hire self-taught engineers, folks with **uncommon backgrounds** or major career shifts.

These people are often brilliant and motivated, but **not big on credentials**. So large companies overlook them.

Large companies have to setup **predictable hiring processes**, so this kind of talent falls through the cracks.
