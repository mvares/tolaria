# Build alignment with candidates via the job post

Most of this alignment, though, can be built before-hand via the job post, with massive savings of time and energy by everyone involved.

A **good job post** should already be informative on these points, in order to 1) reduce the number of candidates who are unfit, and 2) simplify the negotiation later on.

- Can you help with relocation? Great! Let them know in the job post.

- Do you have a streamlined, no-stress interview process? Awesome, describe how it works in advance.

- The same goes for salary. Include a range into the post to 1) get more applications, and 2) make negotiation simpler.
