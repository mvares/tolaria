# Work with vendors is based on trust

Whether you realize it or not, relationships with software agencies and partners are always based on trust. Building trust reduces overhead and makes you go faster. This is natural and healthy because *work relationships* are first and foremost ***relationships***. 

You can't replace trust with more contract clauses.

When you find yourself putting more boundaries into a contract, ask yourself: am I doing this because of a real need, or because of lack of trust? If it is the latter, focus on **building trust** first, so you can **reduce complexity** later.
