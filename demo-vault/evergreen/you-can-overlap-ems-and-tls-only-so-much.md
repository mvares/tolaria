# You can overlap EMs and TLs only so much

⚖️ Managers or Tech Leads? ⚖️ 

While there is wide consensus around the *dual *career track model for managers and ICs, it is also common in many teams to have senior devs taking on *both* tech and some managerial duties.

This may happen because 1) your headcount is small, and/or 2) you have people who can pull this off — yes, they do exist.

Of course, the more your team grows, the harder it gets to conflate the two roles. 

Growth brings extra burden on managers when it comes to hiring, onboarding, perf reviews, promotions, etc. You get to a point where having dedicated managers just makes more sense.

In general, the relationship between Tech Leads and EMs is complicated and there is no one size fits all solution. So, always listen to the advice you can find online (even mine!) but decide for yourself based on what feels write for your team
