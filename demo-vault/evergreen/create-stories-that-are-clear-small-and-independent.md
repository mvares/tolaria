# Create stories that are clear, small and independent

I believe the single most important factor for good engineering planning is to create good stories.

A good story is:

- 👌 **Clear** — requirements are well-written and engineers knows how to build them. To hit this consistently, both PM and engineers need to be involved in creating stories — to refine, correct, and split them.

- 🤏 **Small **— it should never require more than a few days of work. You should always work in small batches

- 🤝 **Independent** — it can be deployed in isolation. If it is part of a larger release, you should be able to deploy it anyway and put it behind a feature flag.
