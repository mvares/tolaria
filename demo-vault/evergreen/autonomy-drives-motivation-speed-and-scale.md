# Autonomy drives motivation, speed and scale

Companies should foster **autonomy **in teams and individuals for two reasons:

- It drives **motivation** (it is one of [Untitled](https://www.notion.so/3b3eb7b8f63c42479f40f1af911bda4b))

- It reduces the need for **communication**, allowing people to do more work, faster.

This is true at all levels:

- **Teams** should be able to work autonomously on their product space.

- **Individuals** should be able to make autonomous decisions and work.

Companies should **default to trust **because autonomy is *so *important
