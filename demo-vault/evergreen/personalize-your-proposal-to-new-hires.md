# Personalize your proposal to new hires

As a startup/small company, one of the cards you can play to win at hiring against big orgs is to personalize your proposal to candidates.

When interviewing somebody, consider how they would fit into your team.

Why are you excited to work precisely with them?

Make them feel part of something great, create anticipation towards the work you can do together.

In your proposal, tell the story of how this particular person, with their personal story, background and skills, will fit your team and thrive into it.

People fear being just a number in a large, faceless organization — and that's where you can fire back ❤️
