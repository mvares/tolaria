# Sponsorship is underrated

Sponsors are those people who **do** practical things to help you. 

They might advocate for your promotion, approve budget for your project, or intro you to useful people. 

While mentoring is about giving advice, sponsoring is about empowering and **giving opportunities**. As a manager, you can sponsor your report by suggest them as a good lead for a new project, or to give a talk about something they are expert about.
