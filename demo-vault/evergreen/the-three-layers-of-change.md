# The Three Layers of Change

Think about change as being made of three layers:

1. 🌟 **Identity** — is the person you want to become. It is the improved version of you that you look forward to, which unlocks the *why* you are doing this.

1. 🔄 **Processes** — are you systems, or habits, to bring you closer to your improved identity, and make the outcomes happen.

1. 🏆 **Outcomes** — are the specific things you want to *achieve*. It can only work in combination with the other two.

James Clear argues that the strongest habits are **identity-based**, rather than **outcome-based**.

Outcome-based habits tend to be weaker and temporary, while identity is what you need to play *infinite games* and make things stick.
