# If candidate rejects your final offer it is a process fail

Converting candidates happens at the end, but is the **result of the whole process**.

So, what are the most important factors for this? Let me start with the one that shouldn’t be: **the offer**.

If you fail to convert a candidate because your offer doesn’t meet their expectations, it is a massive fail of your process.

You have two easy entry points to cover the compensation range early on: the job post and the screening interview. These are where you need to make sure that mutual expectations can be met.
