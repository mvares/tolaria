# Use story count to calculate velocity and effort

When people ask me how to create estimates for projects, my answer is often not what they expect:

1. Create good stories

1. Count how many you can do in a cycle (e.g. two weeks)

1. Estimate how many cycles you need to complete the project.

That's it! 90% of the times you don't need anything more than that.

Here is why 👇

Create good stories means [Untitled](https://www.notion.so/d2bf721de15d4de1ab1c8ff0e8916525). 

When you do it right, stories don't have much variance in size. You may still quickly "triage" them in a few t-shirt sizes (S, M, L), but you shouldn't expect stories that are 10x others.

If stories are good, over time differences in size get evened out, and the count measure becomes just as accurate as measuring story points. But way simpler.
