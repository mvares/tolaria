# Intentionally allocating engineering time is a win

Intentionally allocating engineering time across various categories (e.g. KTLO vs improvements vs new features) has a strong positive correlation with several traits:

- +22% time spent as it was planned (55% vs 45%)

- +24% projects delivered on time (62% vs 50%)

- +14.7% happiness about dev practices (3.57 vs 3.11, out of 5)

- +6.7% focus time (3.16 vs 2.96, out of 5)

![image](../assets/197bdf02815c80e3.png)

This is something we often [discussed on the newsletter](https://refactoring.fm/p/engineering-investments?utm_source=publication-search) and we were already big fans of, but we were surprised by how strong the correlation is.
