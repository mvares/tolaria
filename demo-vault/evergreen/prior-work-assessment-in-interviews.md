# Prior work assessment in interviews

Some companies ask candidates for past work samples to evaluate, in alternative to some of the steps above. This approach has upsides and downsides.

**Upsides:**

- It removes **stress** from the candidate.

- You evaluate stuff from a **real-world** setting.

**Downsides:**

- Most candidates do not have an **open-source** presence, which makes the approach unfeasible.

- It may require **more time** and effort from the people on your team.

Overall it is an interesting strategy, but unless your company is focused on open-source and wants to hire people from that space, I can’t recommend it 100%. It makes the process heavier and trickier in many ways — I would not choose it over a good *take-home *or *pair programming* session.
