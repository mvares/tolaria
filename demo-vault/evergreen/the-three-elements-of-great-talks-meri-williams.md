# The three elements of great talks (Meri Williams)

After 10+ years of curating LeadDev conferences, Meri has identified what makes talks truly memorable and actionable.

#### The Three Elements of Great Talks

- 📖 **Personal story or case study** — real experience that grounds the content

- 🧠 **Abstracted framework** — theory that can be applied broadly

- ⚡ **Practical application** — clear takeaways that audiences can implement
