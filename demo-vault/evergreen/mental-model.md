# Mental Model

Mental models are **frameworks** for thought (lenses), they are **shortcuts** to create good ideas and good decisions.

We can go far without them, relying on our own **intuition** and process, but there are concepts that are counterintuitive and it would be hard to come up with them every time if we had not stored them as a "mental model".

The more **counterintuitive** the thinking is, the more useful is the mental model.

#### How Mental Models are born

- Mental models are born out of **reflection** on our own thinking. 

- We don't just focus on our conclusions, but also on *why *we got there, what is the **process** we followed. 

- When we are able to **formalize** and generalize it properly, we get to a *mental model*.

- Charlie Munger
