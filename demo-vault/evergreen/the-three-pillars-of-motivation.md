# The Three Pillars of Motivation

**The Three Pillars **theory is a [Untitled](https://www.notion.so/a826283d0685429e97d81ba0251e89ff) proposed by [Untitled](https://www.notion.so/cb0ca30953f84e6a92c485fd0b6b87b5) that identifies the core elements that drive our motivation:

1. **Autonomy:** *the desire to be self directed*

1. **Mastery: ***the desire to improve our craft*

1. **Purpose: ***the desire to have a positive impact*

---

Drive by David Pink changed the way I think about engagement and motivation.

He identifies three core elements behind it:

🏃‍♂️ Autonomy — the desire to be self-directed
🔨 Mastery — the desire to improve our craft
🌟 Purpose — the desire to have a positive impact

For how simple as this theory is, I have always found a direct connection between those three elements and how great tech teams behave.

Likewise, when people are disengaged or unhappy about work, you can most often trace it back to one (or more) of those.
