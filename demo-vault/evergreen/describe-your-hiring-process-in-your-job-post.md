# Describe your hiring process in your job post

One of the most overlooked ways to make a job post more effective is to include a precise description of your hiring process.

Describing the process makes the candidate **prepare** for it and **commit** to it. And the more the candidate is committed, the more she will be keen to join your company in the end.

A few tips about your hiring process 👇

- 🤏 **Make it short** — no more than 3-4 steps. You don't need more, and speed always wins deals

- ⏱️ **Include times **— provide an expectation of how long each step will take, from replying to the application, to the final hire.

- 📖 **Write a guide** — let candidates know what to expect from interviews. Give them the chance to prepare as if they were studying for a test. Make them feel invested in the process.
