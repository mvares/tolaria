# Brainstorm with eng. leaders to come up with tech debt initiatives

These proposals need to be sourced, collected, and evaluated multiple times a year. Collaboration is crucial here, too, as the best ideas to reduce debt might span multiple teams.

Hold brainstorming sessions on a quarterly basis with other **engineering leaders** and keep a shared backlog of such initiatives.
