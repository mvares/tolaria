# Visita dalla Nonna — 2024-05-26
Visita mensile alla nonna a Lecco. Pranzo con risotto.

## Notes
- Late May warmth, the lake was sparkling and Lecco felt almost Mediterranean
- Nonna made vitello tonnato as antipasto and then risotto ai funghi porcini
- She mentioned her knees are bothering her on the stairs — gently suggested she see the doctor
- "Non ho bisogno del dottore, ho bisogno che vieni piu spesso" — classic nonna deflection
- Left feeling both happy and a little worried; she's strong but she's getting older
