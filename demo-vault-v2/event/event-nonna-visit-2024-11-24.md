# Visita dalla Nonna — 2024-11-24
Visita mensile alla nonna a Lecco. Pranzo con risotto.

## Notes
- Birthday visit — the whole family came: mamma, papa, Elena, and me
- Nonna pretended to be surprised even though she definitely heard us coordinating on the phone
- Mamma brought the torta della nonna she baked, nonna said "e buona ma non come la mia"
- She blew out the candles with a wish she wouldn't tell us, but her eyes were shining
- The scarf mamma knitted fit perfectly — nonna wore it the rest of the day, even indoors
