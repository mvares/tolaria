# Visita dalla Nonna — 2025-01-26
Visita mensile alla nonna a Lecco. Pranzo con risotto.

## Notes
- Crisp January day, fresh snow on the peaks above Lecco — the drive was scenic
- Nonna made risotto al tastasal, the traditional pork-and-rice dish she only makes in deep winter
- She's been re-reading old novels — currently on "I Promessi Sposi," set right here in Lecco
- We talked about nonno, how he used to take her dancing at the circolo on Saturday nights
- Left with a container of her ribollita and strict instructions to eat it within two days
