# Visita dalla Nonna — 2024-03-31
Visita mensile alla nonna a Lecco. Pranzo con risotto.

## Notes
- Easter Sunday — the whole family gathered at nonna's for pranzo di Pasqua
- She made her legendary lasagna, layer upon layer of ragu, besciamella, and love
- The colomba she baked turned out perfectly — crusty on top, soft inside, she was so proud
- Nonna wore her best dress and her mother's brooch, she looked radiant
- After lunch we walked by the lake, the sun was warm and the mountains were snow-capped — a perfect Easter
