# Visita dalla Nonna — 2024-10-27
Visita mensile alla nonna a Lecco. Pranzo con risotto.

## Notes
- Foggy October drive, the lake appeared and disappeared through the mist — mysterious and beautiful
- Nonna prepared casoncelli alla bergamasca with burnt butter and sage, rich and comforting
- She asked about work — tried to explain what I do with computers, she nodded politely and said "l'importante e che ti piace"
- Her birthday is next month — she says she doesn't want a party but we're planning one anyway
- The house smelled of cinnamon and apples, she'd been baking all morning
